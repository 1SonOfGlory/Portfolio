3-DOF SCARA ITX Robotic Arm

Robotics · Computer Vision · Python/C++ · ARM Cortex-M · Embedded Systems · CAD

A fully functional 3 Degree-of-Freedom Selective Compliance Articulated Robot Arm (SCARA) designed and built from the ground up. The project spanned the entire engineering pipeline: from the mathematical derivation of forward and inverse kinematics, through detailed CAD modelling, to embedded programming of servo motors and the final physical assembly. The robot's embedded logic was written in C++ and powered by an NXP FRDM-KL25Z ARM Cortex-M0+ microcontroller, providing robust real-time control. Equipped with a camera module and an electromagnet at the base of the rack and pinion mechanism, the arm was engineered for intelligent, computer-vision guided pick-and-place operations. Utilizing OpenCV and custom Computer Vision algorithms written in Python, the system autonomously identifies specific colors and sorts metallic objects from a mixed environment, demonstrating practical expertise in applied AI/ML and core industrial automation principles.

IntelliTap: Intelligent Contactless Tap Head

COVID-19 Response · IoT · Proximity Sensing · Hygiene Engineering

In 2021, at the peak of COVID, we realized it made no sense to scrub your hands only to touch the same dirty tap everyone else uses to turn the water off. You basically re-contaminate yourself immediately.

We designed IntelliTap to solve this. It is a portable, touchless tap head that uses sensors to fix the problem.

The Essentials:

  • Quick Setup: It clips onto any standard manual faucet.
  • Hands-Free: Ultrasonic sensors detect your hands and trigger a water valve.

The Result: Wash your hands, walk away, and never touch the hardware.
